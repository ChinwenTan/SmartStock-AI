## 📌 Project Overview

SyncBite AI is an AI-enabled inventory management and decision intelligence platform designed to help Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) improve stock control, reduce wastage, and make smarter purchasing decisions. Many small businesses still rely on spreadsheets, handwritten notes, or personal judgement when managing inventory. This often causes stock shortages, overstocking, wastage, and poor business decisions. 

SyncBite AI solves this problem by combining real-time inventory tracking, demand forecasting, and AI-powered recommendations.

## 🚀 Key Features
- Smart Inventory Management  
- Demand Forecasting using AI  
- Low Stock Alerts  
- Cost & Profit Insights  
- Dashboard Analytics  
- AI Decision Recommendations  
- Multi-user Staff Access  
- Secure Login System  

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Small businesses often face:

- Inaccurate stock records  
- Overstocking or understocking  
- Food/product wastage  
- Manual decision making  
- No forecasting tools  
- Time-consuming operations  

SyncBite AI transforms traditional businesses into data-driven businesses.

## 💡 Our Solution

SyncBite AI uses AI and analytics to:

- Predict future stock demand  
- Recommend reorder quantities  
- Detect slow-moving products  
- Improve purchasing decisions  
- Reduce waste and cost  
- Increase operational efficiency
