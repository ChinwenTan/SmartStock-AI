## 📌 Project Overview

SyncBite-AI is an AI-enabled inventory management and decision intelligence platform designed to help campus-adjacent Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) improve stock control, reduce wastage, and make smarter purchasing decisions. Also, bridge the gap between their inventory and dynamic campus events. Many small businesses still rely entirely on gut feeling to forecast daily demand.This leads to massive perishable food waste and unrecoverable sunk costs when sudden anomalies happen—such as semester break week, unannounced online classes or sudden thunderstorms. Small businesses who relies on manually writing noted also often causes stock shortages, overstocking, wastage, and poor business decisions. 

SyncBite-AI solves this by synchronizing external campus data with internal inventory, generating real-time, AI-driven business pivots to protect profit margins.

- ## 🚀 Core Features
* **Mobile-First Bento Dashboard:** Scannable, high-density UI for tracking real-time revenue and waste risk.
* **Campus Intelligence Hub:** Live syncing of university academic calendars and weather anomaly alerts.
* **Smart Inventory Risk-Tiering:** Auto-classifies perishable stock into High, Medium, or Safe tiers based on exact shelf-life mathematics.
* **Multimodal AI Assistant:** Log inventory quickly via text, voice notes, or photos of your display case.
* **Z.AI Strategy Engine:** Zero-shot prompting that instantly generates specific promotional strategies (e.g., "Rainy Day Delivery Bundle") to recover at-risk capital.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Small businesses often face:

- Disconnection from live campus dynamics (exams, weather, schedule shifts).
- Inaccurate stock records  
- Overstocking or understocking  
- Food/product wastage  
- Manual decision making  
- No forecasting tools  
- Time-consuming operations  

SyncBite-AI shifts merchants from *reactive waste* to *proactive AI-driven recovery*. By acting as the digital brain for the merchant, the app detects campus disruptions, analyzes expiring inventory, and automatically recommends profitable pivot strategies with predicted financial recovery metrics.
